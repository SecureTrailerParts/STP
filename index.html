About

SECURE TRAILER PARTS

Secure Trailer Parts (STP) was founded in 2006 as an independent and family owned business.The company provides services for the automotive OE and aftermarket, trailer, towing and agri-cultural markets throughout Europe, United States, North America, South America and Australia.STP is a modern group company with high output capacity. We have all the advantages of a small company and provide excellent customer service. Production is mostly within the company, from product development to completion. We also have experts for doing sourcing and we have a highly strict incoming inspection procedure.STP's product line includes a wide range of trailer connectors, trailer cable assemblies, trailer lamps, trailer lighting boards, trailer locks and trailer parts. With specialized experience and skilled workforce, we have acquired sound know-how with regard to materials and manufacturing of products for today's demanding markets.STP aims to continue to focus on our key market areas with new products, new ranges, whilst maintaining our high level of customer service. We will always deliver high-quality and zero-defect products. We will not only meet customers' needs, but also assist customers in maximizing sales and improving profits.
