PLUG

Article no : ST101
DIN / ISO : 1724 N-Typ
Number of Pins : 7 terminal
Mounting Type : Bolt Connection
Material : Aluminium
Voltage (V) : 12V
Info : with rubber grommet
  • Information

Plug connection according to DIN/lSO 1724:

This 7-pin connector system is the most widely used connection between towing vehicles and the trailers for vehicles with 12V electrical systems. They are mostly used in the agricultural and automotive sectors.

Plug connection according to DIN/ISO 11446:

As opposed to the 7-pin system, this 13. pin plug connection for 12V vehicle elec- trical systems can cover all lighting and auxiliary functions with one plug. Further benefits are increased impermeability, stability, contact reliability and a simple assembly.Today,the system is mostly used in the automotive sector./p>

WeSt plug connection according to NEN 6120:

This system can be used with both the 7 and 13-pin 12V connection of towing ve- hicles and trailers. Construction of additi- onal contacts according to lSO 1724 + 6.
